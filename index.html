Conquery is a 2d top-down adventure game. Capture all the planets to win.

Controls

Use your mouse to move the player. Hold space to capture planets.

Story

It's 2503 AD. A maniac wants to capture the world using his machine, Conquery. He thinks the world his small enough for him to capture. Conquery doesn't have any attack units built in. But, Conquery can capture planets which would in turn act as your turrets. Can you capture the world controlling Conquery?
